# 🏪 Store Knowledge Base

## 1. Introduction
Welcome to our online store. We are committed to providing high-quality products and excellent customer service. This knowledge base contains important information about shipping, returns, payments, and customer support.

---

## 2. Shipping Policy

### 2.1 Delivery Time
Shipping usually takes between 3 to 5 business days for domestic orders. International shipping may take 7 to 14 business days depending on the destination.

### 2.2 Shipping Fees
- Free shipping is available for orders over $50.
- Orders below $50 will incur a standard shipping fee of $5.

### 2.3 Order Processing
All orders are processed within 24 hours after payment confirmation. Orders placed on weekends or holidays will be processed on the next business day.

---

## 3. Return & Refund Policy

### 3.1 Return Conditions
Customers can return items within 7 days of receiving the product. To be eligible for a return:
- The item must be unused
- The item must be in its original packaging
- A receipt or proof of purchase is required

### 3.2 Refund Process
Once the returned item is received and inspected:
- Refunds are processed within 5 business days
- The refund will be issued to the original payment method

### 3.3 Non-returnable Items
- Gift cards
- Downloadable software products
- Personal care items

---

## 4. Payment Methods

### 4.1 Accepted Payments
We accept the following payment methods:
- Credit cards (Visa, MasterCard)

- E-wallets (Momo, ZaloPay)

### 4.2 Cash on Delivery (COD)
Cash on Delivery is available in selected areas. Customers must confirm orders via phone before delivery.

### 4.3 Payment Security
All transactions are secured using encryption technology to ensure customer data safety.
